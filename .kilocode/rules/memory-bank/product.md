# Product Context: qbit.zone Landing Page

## Why This Landing Page Exists

A pre-launch landing page for qbit.zone - a future platform for quantum computing algorithms. The site positions the domain as a thought leader in quantum computing while collecting visitor interest.

## Problems It Solves

1. **Domain Activation**: Transform purchased domain into an active presence
2. **Thought Leadership**: Establish credibility in quantum computing space
3. **Audience Building**: Collect early interest for future platform
4. **SEO Foundation**: Create search-indexable content for quantum computing keywords

## User Experience Goals

1. **Immediate comprehension** - Visitors understand qbit.zone focus within 5 seconds
2. **Engagement** - Users scroll through all sections, drawn by compelling visuals
3. **Trust building** - Real news content shows active curation and expertise
4. **Action** - Clear call-to-action for future sign-ups

## Content Strategy

- Use real, current quantum computing news (March 2026)
- Balance technical accuracy with accessibility
- Highlight both opportunities and challenges
- Emphasize security implications (post-quantum cryptography)
